About Us

At Furni, we believe that great furniture should be accessible to everyone. Since 2010, we've been crafting beautiful, functional pieces that transform houses into homes.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create furniture that combines timeless design with exceptional quality, all at prices that won't break the bank.

Our Story

Founded in a small workshop in Portland, Oregon, Furni began as a passion project between two friends who shared a love for woodworking and design. What started as crafting custom pieces for friends and family quickly grew into something much bigger.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted name in furniture, serving customers across the country with our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Every piece in our collection is designed with care, built to last, and created to bring joy to your everyday life. We believe that the right furniture can transform a space and create memories that last a lifetime.
